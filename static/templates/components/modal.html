How it works?
Choose your pokemon and entire your email to receive your secret API key. Send a HTTP POST request using that secret key to have your pokemon appear on the wall. Limited space available. See the following example: curl --location 'https://pokewallapp.azurewebsites.net/api/v1/pokemon/' \ --header 'Content-Type: application/json' \ --data '{ "name": "YOUR NAME", "key": "API KEY", "pokemon": "POKEMON NAME", "url": "WEBSITE URL" }'
